IAT 355 — Video Game Sales (Vega-Lite)

Four core explorations + follow-up charts, built in Vega-Lite and embedded with JavaScript.

V1) Global Sales by Genre and Platform

Stacked bar chart showing total global sales by genre with platform contributions (top 10 platforms to reduce clutter).

V2) Sales Trends Over Time — PSP Action

Line chart of aggregated global sales over time for PSP Action games.

V3) Regional Sales Distribution — PSP Action

Pie chart showing sales share across NA, EU, JP, and Other.

V4) Company Dominance in Action Games

Bar chart grouping platforms into company ecosystems (Sony/Nintendo/Microsoft/Other).

Follow-up 1) Average Sales per Game — Genre × Platform

Mean global sales per title to compare performance vs volume (top 10 platforms).

Follow-up 2) PSP Action — Sales vs Releases Over Time

Two lines: total sales per year vs number of releases per year.

Follow-up 3) PSP — Regional Share by Genre

100% stacked bars comparing regional dependence across genres.

Follow-up 4) Company Sales Across Genres

Total global sales by genre, grouped by company ecosystem.
